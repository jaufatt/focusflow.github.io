Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusFlow handles information when you use the app. The goal is to be clear, simple, and respectful of your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data – basic information about how you use the app (e.g. which features are used).
  • Crash logs – technical information if the app crashes, used to improve stability.
  • Text you enter – content you type into the AI Coach, used to generate responses.

How We Use This Information

  • To keep the app running reliably and improve features.
  • To generate AI responses and support your experience.
  • To understand general usage patterns in an aggregated way.

Data Sharing

FocusFlow does not sell your personal data. Limited information may be shared with third‑party services such as analytics or crash reporting tools, strictly for the purpose of improving performance and stability.
