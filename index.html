What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a calm AI companion that helps you stay focused, unwind after long days, and gently check in with how you’re really doing. It’s designed to feel like a safe, quiet space—not another noisy productivity app.

What you can do with FocusFlow

  • Talk to your AI Coach – Share how you’re feeling or what you’re working on and get warm, supportive guidance.
  • Start focus sessions – Use deep‑work timers to stay on track without feeling overwhelmed.
  • Take a moment to relax – Short, calming experiences to help you slow down, breathe, and reset.
  • Daily check‑ins – Reflect on how you’re doing emotionally, not just how much you got done.
  • See your patterns – Notice trends in your focus, mood, and habits over time.
